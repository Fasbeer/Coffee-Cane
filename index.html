About us


For health conscious young adults and professionals in Central London who are looking for healthier alternatives to their everyday coffee, Coffee & Cane will be able to quench their thirst in a unique way.


Nowadays, people seek food and drinks which are, fresh, natural and minimally processed. With these in consideration, we at Coffee and Cane, are taking the initiative to please your sweet tooth using a healthy natural sweetener -Sugarcane Juice.
